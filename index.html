Classic Car Rallies for the Fun Of It

The first event for Two Lane Touring Rallies is to make a 3-day trip from the wonderful Lane Motor Museum just outside of Nashville, TN, to the annual Hilton Head Concours d'Elegance in Hilton Head, SC. We'll be avoiding highways at all cost, taking twisty back roads through the mountains and beautiful countryside. Join us for an epic adventure!

What kinds of cars are welcome on a Two Lane Touring Rally?

We love all kinds of cars. If what you're driving is fun and interesting, it's welcome on the tour! It's always a good idea to be comfortable with your car driving hundreds of miles, with a bit of that at highway speeds. The most important part is that everyone has fun, both behind the wheel and on two feet.
